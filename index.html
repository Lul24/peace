About Thon Met

Our Mission

Thon Met African Peace Foundation, Inc. is a grassroots nonprofit organization committed to uplifting South Sudanese refugee communities through targeted initiatives that address the root causes of poverty and underdevelopment. Our work spans education, healthcare, clean drinking water, and agriculture, aiming to create a comprehensive impact.

Who We Are?

At the Thon Met African Peace Foundation, Inc., we are dedicated to supporting South Sudanese refugees who have been forced to flee their homes and find themselves in some of the most vulnerable refugee camps in South Sudan, Ethiopia, Sudan, and Kenya. Our mission is rooted in compassion and respect for all individuals, driven by the heartfelt belief that everyone deserves the opportunity to live a life filled with safety, hope, and the chance to thrive. We understand South Sudanese refugees' struggles poverty, displacement, conflict, and barriers to essential services and we are here to help. Our work goes beyond merely providing immediate relief; we strive to empower individuals, restore their dignity, and build a foundation for a more just and sustainable future.

Through meaningful partnerships and active community involvement, we provide vital resources such as food security, access to clean water, healthcare, education, and transportation. We listen closely to the needs of the communities we serve and adapt our methods to ensure their voices are heard and prioritized in our initiatives. Together, we are committed to nurturing resilient communities and uplifting lives, one small step at a time, guided by empathy and a shared commitment to a brighter future.

Our Programs

Through our diverse initiatives, we address key challenges facing African communities and work toward sustainable solutions.

Education

Empowering youth through access to quality education, learning resources, and scholarship programs for underprivileged students.

Healthcare

Providing essential medical services, health education, and promoting wellness practices in rural and underserved communities.

Sustainability

We focus on enhancing water access, conserving biodiversity, and promoting sustainable agriculture for South Sudanese refugee families in the Gambella region.
